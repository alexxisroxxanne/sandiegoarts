The US Grant, a Luxury Collection Hotel, San Diego, United States

326 Broadway, San Diego, CA 92101, United States

http://www.usgrant.net/

Enrich your travels with a gateway to a timeless San Diego Hotel: A national icon since 1910, THE US GRANT is situated in the city's lively and historic downtown Gaslamp Quarter. The hotel is just steps from shopping, dining, and entertainment, and a short drive from beaches, and family attractions.

Our San Diego US Grant hotel entices guests with a dazzling array activities within walking distance. Experience year-round performances at the many theaters in the area, dine upon an eclectic array of international cuisine, taste San Diego craft beers and more. 

Location

San Diego US Grant hotel is located in historic, vivacious Gaslamp Quarter, which entices with a dazzling array of dining, shopping, nightlife and entertainment venues for all ages. Explore the city's young heritage and learn about early settlers, including Alonzo Horton and Wyatt Earp at the Gaslamp Quarter Historical Foundation. Your journey can include daytime exploratory tours or an experience within the region's energetic restaurants and nightlife.

Evidenced by its ranking as one of the United States' top three tourist and convention destinations, San Diego is a year-round paradise.  Located in sunny Southern California, it boasts an average temperature of 72 degrees, promoting a lifestyle of sports and leisure. From sailing on the San Diego Bay to golfing on a world-famous course to wine tasting in nearby Temecula Valley, the city offers endless options to escape, energize, and discover.

The location of the hotel provides easy access to most venues and attractions in the downtown area, including the Civic Auditorium, Balboa Theatre, Symphony Hall, and the San Diego Convention Center.

Transportation Options

Centrally located in the heart of downtown San Diego, THE US GRANT is easily accessible by air, land, and sea. We recommend flying into the San Diego International Airport-Lindbergh Field (SAN), which services both private jets and most major airlines, with nearly 300 daily departures. Located just 2.7 miles away, the airport is an approximate ten-minute drive from the hotel. If you are traveling by car, please see driving directions, from the airport or any other location.
