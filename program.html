August 5th, 2017


8:00 Registration


I. Science behind Adipose Tissues & Its indications


9:00-10:30 Introduction to the Basic Science of Adipose, Nanofat and PRP

- Understanding the regenerative aspect of adipose tissue

- The location & distribution of adipose tissue in human body

- Nanofat: understanding nanofat and its clinical applications

- Platelet-rich Plasma (PRP): understanding PRP and how it works with adipose tissue


II. Clinical Preparation & Harvesting


10:00-10:30 Syringe Adipose harvesting with local anaesthesia

- Background and mechanisms of action of closed syringe vacuum lipoaspiration

- The Importance of proper pre-tunneling and infiltration for patient comfort, ease of harvesting and minimal bleeding

- Donor Site Selection

- Prep and drape technique


10:30-11:00 Morning Coffee and Tea Break & Networking


11:00-11:45 Adipose and PRP – Adipose Harvesting Protocol

- Instruments and supplies needed for adipose and PRP preparation & harvesting

- Video Demonstration: Step-by-Step protocol for blood draw and adipose harvest in the procedure room – focus on safety, efficacy and simplicity


11:45-12:30 Preparing Adipose for Injection: Processing three sizes of fat based on injectability

- Micro fat – 18g – 20g injectability

- Mille fat – 20g – 25g injectability

- Nano fat – 27g – 30g injectability


12:30-14:00 Lunch Break & Networking


III. How to Use Micro, Mille and Nano Fat for Total Facial and Hand Rejuvenation


14:00-15:00 Video Demonstration: Redeploying fat for structural and dermal rejuvenation

- Injection techniques and applications for each size of fat

- Techniques for smooth results

- Minimizing bruising for minimal patient downtime


15:00-16:00 Video demonstration: Nanofat

- What does nanofat actually do?

- Step-by-step instruction for intradermal injection of nanofat

- Results


16:00-16:30 Afternoon Coffee and Tea Break & Networking


16:30-17:00 Avoiding complications

- Adipose transfer risks and possible complications

- Treating complications

- Post care and treatment to minimize and patient down time
